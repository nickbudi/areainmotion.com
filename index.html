AIM to...

Be Fit

We all set goals for ourselves, it is our “AIM” to challenge you to be the healthiest you that you can be. We encourage this by simply getting moving and exploring healthier alternatives when making food choices. We have fitness mentors, quarterly speakers, a garden club, and dance team which exemplify that simple life movements make a large impact on our whole self.

Give Back

One of our goals for Area in Motion is to give back to our community. We fulfill this through our Annual Canned Food Drive, held every February/March. As an incentive, when you donate 4 cans you receive an AIM wristband. This is our “Cans 4 Bands” initiative. All canned food is donated to our local (Hilton Head and Bluffton) community food banks. When we help feed America we all benefit. We also have other outreach programs which include: Project Unify, Bridges to Broome, and Connections to Cambodia.

Get The Area In Motion

The Community Walk is the true vision of Area in Motion put into action. This 1 hour walk is held annually during the month of March at Hilton Head Island High School Track. Everyone is invited to get out and get moving. We have “Healthy Lane 8” where local vendors such as Hilton Head Regional Hospital, Power House Gym, Island Physical Therapy, and many more offer beneficial information on healthy living. We also have a live DJ (Alan Palchak) and healthy prizes. It is a positive don’t miss event!

About Us

Born in California, a well traveled Marine brat, and now a Hilton Head Island resident, Melissa Ledbetter has a message for all of us. As a vacation visitor since the early 80s, Melissa, better known as Mo to her friends, shares a story common to many of us-she fell in love with the Lowcountry! As a local physical education instructor, she tries to share her commitment to fitness with not just her students, but with the community as a whole. As a person, she has a very strong set of core values that match her dedication to fitness as a part of total well being. In a dual "give back" program, let's learn about Area In Motion-AIM!
